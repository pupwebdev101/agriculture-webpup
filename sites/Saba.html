SABA

Saba is the kind of banana fruit used generally for cooking but may be eaten in raw form. The banana form is the most important variety of banana used in the Philippines cuisine. Also addressed as Cardaba banana at times, the banana falls under Saba variety or within Saba group.

Saba banana carries robust pseudostems and are large enough to reach the height of 20-30 feet. Well, the trunk can carry diameter of 0.91 meter or up to 3 feet. The color of the leaves and trunk may be blue and green in color.

Saba Banana gets ready within 150 to 180 days of planting and it can be harvested after that. Other banana varieties dont take long as Saba banana. The expected yield may be between 26-38 kg every bunch.

Saba banana is distinctly angular and squarish which may be cooked. We all love to eat the banana as it tastes really awesome. Apart from tasting great, the banana offers a range of health benefits.
